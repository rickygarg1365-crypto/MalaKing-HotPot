Getting Here

By Car

Located in the City Parkway Plaza, we're easily accessible from Highway 99 and King George Boulevard. Free parking is available in the plaza.

From Vancouver: Take Highway 99 South, exit at King George Boulevard

From Richmond: Take Highway 99 North, exit at 104th Avenue

By Transit

We're conveniently located near several bus routes and the Surrey Central SkyTrain station.

Bus Routes: 321, 323, 324, 325

SkyTrain: Surrey Central Station (10 min walk)

Walking

If you're in the Surrey Central area, we're just a short walk away from the mall and other attractions.

From Surrey Central Mall: 5 minute walk

From King George Station: 8 minute walk
