Our Story

Bringing the authentic hot pot experience to Surrey

Welcome to Mala King Hotpot

Founded with a passion for authentic Asian cuisine and a vision to revolutionize the dining experience, Mala King Hotpot has become Surrey's premier destination for interactive hot pot dining.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to bring the traditional hot pot experience to modern diners who crave both authenticity and convenience. We believe that great food should be accessible, customizable, and above all, delicious.

At Mala King, we've reimagined the traditional hot pot concept with our innovative "Weigh to Pay" system. This unique approach allows our guests to create their perfect meal by selecting from over 100 fresh ingredients and choosing from our signature broths, all while only paying for what they love.

Our Philosophy

Fresh & Quality

We source only the freshest ingredients daily, ensuring every bite is packed with flavor and nutrition. Our commitment to quality is reflected in every dish we serve.

Your Way

We believe that great food should be personal. Our unique system allows you to customize every aspect of your meal, from ingredients to broth, creating the perfect hot pot experience.

Authentic Flavors

Our broths are crafted using traditional recipes and premium ingredients, bringing authentic Asian flavors to every bowl. From spicy Szechuan to comforting miso, we have something for every palate.

Value & Transparency

Our "Weigh to Pay" system ensures you only pay for what you choose. No hidden costs, no surprises - just honest pricing for honest portions.
