Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We accept reservations for groups of 6 or more. Please call us at (604) 496-3148 or use our contact form above to make a reservation.

How does the "Weigh to Pay" system work?

You select your ingredients from our fresh selection, choose your broth, and we weigh your bowl. You only pay for what you choose, making it perfect for any appetite or budget.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We offer catering for events and parties. Please contact us with your requirements and we'll be happy to create a custom package for you.

Are you open on holidays?

We're open most holidays! Please call ahead to confirm our hours on specific holidays, as they may vary.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer a wide variety of vegetarian ingredients and our Mushroom Vegan Broth is a popular choice among vegetarian diners.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available in the plaza where we're located. The parking lot is well-lit and monitored for your safety.
